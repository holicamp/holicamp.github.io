😷  COVID19 Health and safety

Holicamp takes all measures to avoid the risk of COVID19 propagation for both outdoor and indoor camps:

  1. Temperature reading at the start of the day for every child
  2. Washing hands at the start/end of the day
  3. Washing hands after each activity
  4. Children kept 2 meters apart during briefings and lunch breaks
  5. Adapted activities according to the situation

⚠️  Cancelation warning

One or more days could be canceled and refunded depending on the ongoing situation and potentially new regulations.
